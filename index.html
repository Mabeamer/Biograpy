What is Road To Hire?

Road To Hire is a program that takes young motivated adults that think that college isnt for them and puts them into a 4 month long training course, the end goal of the program is to help get them started in the programming world.

There are two tracks people can take, Tech and Sales. The Tech side spends a normal work day learning the fundamentals of programming and how to code in multiple languages.

The Sales track spends their work day learning how to speak on the phone and the proper etiquette over the phone with customers
